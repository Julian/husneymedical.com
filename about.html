Jack Husney, MD is a Gastroenterologist and leading expert in diseases related to the stomach, bowels and liver and nutrition. He holds Triple Board Certification in his fields.

Dr. Husney, serves as an Honorary NYPD Surgeon. As an Honorary Police Surgeon, he is called to assist injured members of the NYPD and their immediate family members when they are in need of medical treatment.

Dr. Husney completed his Gastroenterology and Hepatology training at the premier Mount Sinai Hospital In New York City. In addition, he continued his training at Weill Cornell Medical Center where he trained in the field of non-surgical weight loss procedures.
